Caleb Harrison

UNC Chapel Hill • Graduate Student

I am a fourth-year graduate student at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. Within philosophy, my main interests lie in ethics (normative and applied), social and political philosophy, and moral psychology. I am writing my dissertation on trust, with a specific focus on trusting attitudes in individuals, and their direction of these attitudes toward groups. I wrote my MA thesis on justice in Adam Smith's Theory of Moral Sentiments.

Prior to coming to Chapel Hill, I completed my BA at St. Olaf College.
