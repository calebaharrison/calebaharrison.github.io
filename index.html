Caleb Harrison

UNC Chapel Hill • Graduate Student

I am a third year graduate student at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. Prior to coming to Chapel Hill, I completed my BA at St. Olaf College.

Within philosophy, my main interests lie in social and political philosophy, ethics, and moral psychology. I wrote my MA thesis on justice in Adam Smith's Theory of Moral Sentiments.
